Don't take things at face value and look at alternatives. 
It involves questioning, evaluating and offering better ways of doing things

### How to develop it?

- Develop the habit of asking [[Start with why|why]]? - opens up a world of new opportunities
- If you merely question everything, you become obstructive which makes you a problem. You need to come up with alternatives too.
- When you ask why properly you will find out things like
	- what you are trying to achieve
	- how the current solution came to be
	- the restrictions you are working with
	- who is ultimately responsible for this thing
	- the criteria that would make a successful solution
