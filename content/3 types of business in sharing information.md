1. **Transmission:** This is about sending information through various means, like television, telephone, or the internet. It’s a well-established field, often dominated by large companies that have developed advanced technologies and made significant profits.
2. **Storage:** This involves keeping information safe and accessible, such as through computers and databases. There are many companies, big and small, working on this, with exciting advancements like laser technology that can store huge amounts of data on a single disk.
3. **Understanding:** This is the least developed area. `Understanding connects raw data to useful knowledge and is the true goal of information`. While there are people focused on ==helping others understand information==, there are not many businesses dedicated to this yet. - [[Understanding business]]
