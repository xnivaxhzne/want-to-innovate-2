- The key to figuring out how to remember things is `determining what you don't need to remember.`
- The internet is a small help here as you can quickly get small facts and explanations. You also don't need to remember dates, appointment times, and to-dos as they are synced in the calendar.
- Live your life with the expectation that you will forget all this stuff.
- The key to not stressing about forgetting these things is not even to try to remember them in the first place.
- If it comes naturally to remember something, don't stress about remembering it.
- [[Effective way of helping people remember is to make it unnecessary]]

But even though you don't need to remember all information, you need to remember the key elements and the existence of it. 

Just learn how to find the required information. Knowing how to search for information gives you the freedom to find what you need.

Ex: You don't need to know every words of the English Language. But you should know that there can be a word for the given situation and you can look for it. That is very important. [["We will figure it out" attitude is the best to solve any problems]]
