Published in Dec 2009 Harvard Business Review

* Two identical twins with same brains and natural talents
* Both have been assigned a task: creating a new business venture in a week
* one will stay at home in the week
* but the other spent his whole week by  
	* talking to 10 people - an engineer, a musician, a stay-at-home dad, and designer.
	* visits 3 innovative startups to observe what they do
	* Samples five new to the market products
	* shows a prototype he built to 5 people.
	* Asks the question "what if I tried this?" ....


Who do you bet will come up with the more innovative and doable idea?

**Of course the second Twin**
