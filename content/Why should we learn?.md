Learning is a powerful evolutionary tool that allows organisms to adapt, survive, and thrive in a wide variety of environments. It enhances an individual’s ability to respond to challenges, increases reproductive success, and contributes to the evolution of species by promoting behaviours that are advantageous in changing environments.
