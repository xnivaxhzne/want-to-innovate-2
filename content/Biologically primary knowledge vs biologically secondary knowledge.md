- Biologically secondary knowledge is learnable and teachable.
- Biologically primary knowledge is learnable and not teachable. Because it is usually learned without being explicitly taught.

> Secondary knowledge is culturally invented by humans.

- We have evolved to acquire
- Learned automatically
	- Can't be taught
#### Secondary knowledge/skills:
- It has only become relevant to us within last few thousand years   
- Only needed because culture suggests they are important
- Deliberate efforts are needed
