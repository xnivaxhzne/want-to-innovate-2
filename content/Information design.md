It is the practice of presenting information in a way that fosters an efficient and effective understanding of the information.

Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_design

According to [Per Mollerup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Per_Mollerup "Per Mollerup"), information design is explanation design. 
It explains `facts of the universe` and leads to `knowledge` and` informed action.`
