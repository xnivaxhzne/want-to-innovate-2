- It is a guide of how a film is going to `look`, a way of `visualising` all the shots in a film.
- Its a part of pre-production.
- In Tamil, it is called `காதை விளக்கப்படம்`
- It is used to save resources - time, money, man power.

https://parruu.blogspot.com/p/storyboard.html

### Components of storyboard:
1. Panel - A rectangle represents what the camera see - dimension depends on the aspect ratio. 
2. Arrows - to show camera movements and so on...
