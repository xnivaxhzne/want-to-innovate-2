Innovative entrepreneurs often exhibit the capacity to do two things at once: 

1. they `dive deep into the details` to understand the `subtle nuances` 
2. they fly high to see how the details fit into the [[Big picture|bigger picture]]. 

Synthesising these two views often results in surprising associations.

[[Intuitive type learner and sensing type learner]]

> Once you know what level you're working at, you can zoom in to the appropriate level of detail. Sometimes we need to zoom all the way in on an object. Other times it's more important to zoom out to look at the ecosystem. Being able to zoom in and out as you work is the key to seeing how these levels affect one another.
