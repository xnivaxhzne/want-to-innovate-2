Emotion interacts with [[Human cognition|cognition]] biochemically, bathing the brain with hormones, transmitted either through the bloodstream or through ducts in the brain, modifying the behavior of brain cells.

In tensed state, emotional system releases such hormones which causes to prepare for certain actions.


> Positive emotional state is good for creative thought, but it is not very well suited for getting things done. 

> Negative emotional state is good for focus.
