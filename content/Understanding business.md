Imagine that. Someone in the business of making things understandable: instructions, forms, maps, guidebooks, your body, your country, your world.

- The "understanding business" would focus on helping people grasp fundamental [[Concept|concepts]].
- It would aim to:
	- Make complex data, like the U.S. Census, easier to understand.
	- Provide clear, straightforward annual reports.
	- Simplify insurance forms and loan applications.
	- Help networks and newspapers make global news more comprehensible.
	- Ensure that government information is truly accessible to the public.
	- Help consumers understand their phone and electric bills.
- We need to treat understanding as a business in itself.
