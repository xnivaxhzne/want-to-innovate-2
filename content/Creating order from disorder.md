What is the difference between a bunch of bricks lying on the ground and a house?
What is the difference between a bunch of pieces of wood lying on the ground and a chair?

The former is created order and the later is disorder. Creating a more useful configurations of atoms/electrons/zeros and ones.

When you create order from disorder, you create value - wealth.

You have the raw materials.
You have the assembly process - the know-how, the machines and the effort to put the raw materials together. 

Software is just electron configuration. A physical product is just an atom configuration.
In software, you don't need the raw material atoms. But for physical product, you need.
Know-how is needed for both.
Machine in software case is just computers and servers. But for physical products, it can be a huge infrastructure. 
Both needs the efforts(energy).
