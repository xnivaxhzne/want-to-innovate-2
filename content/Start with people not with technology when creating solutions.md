
[[Spot the humans when you solve a problem]]

Dont start with technology.
Start with people and their aspirations.

Lets say you are designing a music system for people.

Learn how people would like to experience music in their home and design a system around it.
Dont start with a goal of building advanced wireless system.
The system should be the result of asking the question,
> how people want to enjoy music in their home?
