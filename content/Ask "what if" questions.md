> If we did this, what would happen?


Omidyar, a systems analyst, designs `end-user interfaces` from the ground up with `no preconceived way of doing things`.

To do this, Omidyar probes deeply by asking a series of questions that work from a [[Tabula rasa|blank-slate]]

Perspective, such as,
1. `“What would be the cleanest way to solve it?” `

He sees himself as “the devil’s advocate in the room saying things like,
2. `‘What if it really didn’t work this way?` 
3. Or `what if we really did do the opposite of this? `
4. `What would happen then?’`
