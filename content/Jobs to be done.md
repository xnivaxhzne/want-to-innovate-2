`"do a job"` - the terminology used by Claton Christensen in his book - [The Innovator's solution](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/2618.The_Innovator_s_Solution)

- He argued that customers - people and companies - have `"Jobs" that arise regularly` and need to get done.
- When customers aware of a job that they need to get done, they `look around for a product or service` that they can hire.
- When people have a job to do, they set out to` hire something or someone to do the job `as effectively, conveniently, and inexpensively as possible.
