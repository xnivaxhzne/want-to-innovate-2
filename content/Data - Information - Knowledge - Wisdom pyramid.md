# Data:
- Simply a collection of 0s and 1s.
- Pretty useless you do something with it.
- [[Data]]

# Information:
- Once we apply intelligence, we get meaning from it.
- We can describe what is happening and what it represents.
- [[Information]]

# Knowledge:
- When we apply further intelligence to this information in the form of [[Context|context]], past experience, values and other factors, we get knowledge.
- This gives us a more usable [[Understanding|understanding]].
- [[Knowledge]]

# Wisdom:
- This is the holy grail of data. 
- Its been described as the "know-why". 
- Its the understanding that can lead to insightful ideas and better decisions.
