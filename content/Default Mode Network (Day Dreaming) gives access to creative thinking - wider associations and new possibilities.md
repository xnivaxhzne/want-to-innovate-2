### Default Mode Network: 
- The state the brain goes into when it is not focused on a task.
- It was found by observing the blood flow to the front area of the brain when some one is resting.
- It is the state that activates when we are building metal simulations based on past experiences, your knowledge and understanding of the world, imagining the future or envisioning different perspectives and "what if" scenarios.
- This is the state you are in when you start replaying situations in your head.
- It is called `DayDreaming`.
- These drifty, random thoughts are where creative thinking tends to happen.
- In this state, our brain can play with `what if` scenarios and come up with fresh ideas.
- When we focus too sharply on a task, we loose access to  this valuable unfocused thinking.
- This state give access to wiser associations and new possibilities.
