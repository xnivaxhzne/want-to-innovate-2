- The evolution of design to design thinking is the graduation from `thinking about the products or service` to `thinking about the relation between the product and the humans`  and the relation between humans.

- In DT, the emphasis is on thinking and doing, and not just on designing. - [[I hear and I forget, I see and I remember. I do and I understand]]
- [[Outcome of DT may be a product, or a process or a service but more significantly, an experience]]
- If DT is not there, the process of creative problem solving will be more chaotic.
