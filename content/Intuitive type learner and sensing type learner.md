###### Sensing type learner - linear learners:
They don't look around and read from start to end without looking for comparisons.

###### Intuitive type learner - [[Big picture|big picture]] learners or integrative learners:
They look around too much, they have a hard time staying on task. They have a hard time getting through the material. They spend more time on the task and also better memory for details. They, by their very nature, looking for a big picture will become satisfied once they have the big picture and they get bored with the details. Once they get bored, they find difficult to pay attention. So they miss the questions related to details in the test.
