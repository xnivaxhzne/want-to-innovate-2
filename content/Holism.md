The whole is more than the sum of its parts.

Weight is not holistic. It is the opposite. Weight of a bag is the sum of the weight of its parts.

Tastiness of a dish is holistic. It is not sum of the tastiness of the ingredients. A great chef can mix different ingredients to create a dish that transcends the taste of any of the individual ingredients.

[[Hermeneutics circle|Meaning of text]] is even more holistic than taste.
