- domain of reasonably well-articulated problems and a set of well-known solutions
- the emphasis is on `efficiency` and `reducing the variability` of the outcome.
- used in problems around `product manufacturing` and not for design.
- includes easy to solve problems with well tested solutions.

### Techniques:
- [[Six Sigma]]
- [[Kaizen]]
- [[Agile]]
- [[Scrum]]
- [[Root Cause Analysis]]
- [[Five Why Analysis]] and 
- other variants of the lean philosophy.
