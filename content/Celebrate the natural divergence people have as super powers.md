We can call the natural divergence as superpowers.
They are
	- Diversity
	- Background
	- Trauma
	- Illness

- If you make these people identical, you have destroyed the value of their differences.
- We should celebrate the differences and the wonderful potential they offer if we let them.
- It means measuring them by the work they do rather than how they do it.
- Neurodiversity - having a mind that works differently is seen as a plus point rather than an uncomfortable liability.
