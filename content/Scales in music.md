#### Major Scale:

Major Formula - `W W H W W W H` - [[Half step and full step in music]]

Ex: C Major(All white keys) - `C, D, E, F, G, A, B, C`
which has a happy sound.

G Major:
Start with G and follow the above formula
`G, A, B, C, D, E, F#, G`

If you are starting with black key, using flat notation will make the final letter series contains each letter.
