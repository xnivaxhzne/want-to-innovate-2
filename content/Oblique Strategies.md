- It is a card based creative thinking tool.
- The deck consists of a series cards, each containing a cryptic or abstract phrase or instruction designed to stimulate creative thinking and problem-solving.
- It is for encouraging [[Lateral thinking|lateral thinking]] rather than relying on conventional thinking and methods.

#### Examples of phrases on the cards include:

"Emphasize the flaws", "Use an old idea", "Change nothing and continue with immaculate consistency", and "Repetition is a form of change"
