> Brains operate not by [[Logic|logic]] but by pattern recognition. This process is not precise, as is logic and [[Is Maths based on truth?|mathematics]]. Instead, it trades off specificity and precision, if necessary, to increase its range. It is likely, for example, that `early human thought` proceeded by `metaphor`, which, even with the `late acquisition of precise means` such as logic and mathematical thought, continues to be a major source of [[Imagination|imagination]] and [[Creativity|creativity]] in adult life.
