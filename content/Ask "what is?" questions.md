- Disruptive innovators leverage a variety of `what-is` questions to surface `unexpected subtleties`
- These questions work when they reveal what is and `build empathy for how it feels`. 
- Such empathic understanding produces the deep understanding behind better `what-caused `and` what-if` questions.

- What are they really trying to do here?
- What does the body do to these stents and why do they fail?
- Where is the real problem?
- What’s the person trying to achieve?
- What’s most important?
- What’s the real pain point?
