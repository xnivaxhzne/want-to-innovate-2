- [[Prompts for innovation]]

- Have diverse experiences and knowledge.
- These two are nothing but dots.
- Think more about your experiences.
- Curiosity helps here to acquire the dots.
- The dots should stay in the head and not outside.
- Find similarities between two unrelated things to make profound connection.
- Remember that your brain will fight every step of the way to save energy. 
- Experience and knowledge means - the effect and an use for it. 
- Collect them in a way that it should help you when required.
- Try to refine the dots after a period of use.
- Repurpose the effect for a different use.
- Combine/Connect many dots to create new.
- Connecting the unrelated ones yield more novel results.
- Expose yourself to the best things humans have done and steal them.
- Do what you love and have a bold vision to inspire others.
- With your dots, see what others dont.
- Innovation is the process of discovering ways to rearrange the atoms of the world into forms that are unlikely to arise by chance and that happens to be useful.
- Innovation is potentially infinite because even if runs out of new things to do, it can find ways to do the same thing with less energy or more quickly.
- Creative thinking separates us from the rest of the species.
- It helps augment our abilities to achieve things that were not previously possible and let us perform tasks with less efforts.
