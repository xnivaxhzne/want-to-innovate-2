Once you predetermine the categories, it gets difficult to connect ideas from one category to another — let alone multiple. Each idea becomes a _rigid_ part of its category. It’s not a problem to have categories (it guides how you tag notes, for example) — I’m saying the _rigidity_ becomes a problem.
