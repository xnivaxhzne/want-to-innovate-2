
It is a concept of mental building blocks and how they can be used to improve [[Creativity|creativity]] and [[Problem Solving|problem-solving]] skills. By breaking down complex ideas or problems into smaller, more [[Knowledge about something should exist in your brain as a manageable, usable and interactive piece|manageable]] "mental blocks" or "thought legos," individuals `can more easily analyze and manipulate these building blocks` to generate `new ideas and solutions.`

By combining different `mental building blocks in novel ways`, individuals can generate innovative ideas and solutions that might not have been possible through traditional problem-solving methods.

-  One card = one idea
-  You can collect them without worrying about how they fit into a bigger picture
-  You can 
	- group, 
	- shuffle, 
	- shape, 
	- sort, 
	- cluster, 
	- connect, 
	- reorder

Mental Building Blocks can be used in [[Mind map - for Random Connections]] , [[Brain Storming]], [[Analogical Thinking]]
