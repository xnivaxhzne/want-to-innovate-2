> So Valuable thinkers diverge from the norm to access thoughts that lie outside it.

- Conformity can seriously limit people's potential.
- A strong social identity leads to people behave in the same way, same rules, assumptions, same knowledge, same input, same processes, same output.
- If Einstein had been a conformer, the world might have gained an adequate patent clerk, but lost one of its greatest visionaries.
- The conceptual gold lies in a fertile area with right balance of novel and familiarity.
- Right amount of [[Personal divergence|personal divergence]] is a balance. Still function within the group. But doesn't think or operate like the typical member of the group.
