Conceptual system - shapes how we think and interact with the world. 

This means we often understand and experience one thing in terms of another.

Ex: the concept of argument is metaphorically understood as war. 
This metaphor is not just in words but structures how we think and engage in arguments. 

It determines how we perceive reality, making it a central part of human thought.

Conceptual metaphors are mental constructs that influence how we think, act and [[Communication|communicate]]. They extend beyond [[Language|language]] to shape the way we [[Understanding|understand]] the world.
