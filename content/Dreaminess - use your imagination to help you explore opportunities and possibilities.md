### How to develop it?

Without an inspiring problem to work on, your brain will put its imagination to use on romantic fantasies, self reflection or prancing across a rainbow on a unicorn.
But you can put it to work on solving problems too.

- You need to get the information into your head and understand the moving parts of the issue you are dealing with. #insight 
- But once you understand the facts, it can be useful to kick back and let your mind drift.
- Imagine possibilities, picture what might happen if you change certain elements
- Put yourself in other people's shoes to get an understanding of how they might interact with your ideas.
- Write down your thoughts as you go
- If its a good thought, spend time developing it.
- This kind of thinking gives you access to ideas that you just can't get by consciously applying your mind.
- Your office desk is probably not he ideal place for dreaming.
- Do mentally un demanding tasks like washing dishes, having a shower or going for a walk, your brain will wander naturally.
