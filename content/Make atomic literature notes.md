You will end up creating many literature notes from a single fleeting note.  
  
It is important that a literature note follows these simple rules:  
  
1. It is a single idea  
2. It can be understood in isolation  
3. It represents your own understanding, not a paraphrase of someone else's words (with the exception of quotes)  
  
  
Remember, the permanent notes are moving the literature note from the context of a single source material (e.g. a book) to your whole collection of knowledge (from all books, articles, etc).
