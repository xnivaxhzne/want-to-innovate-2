Individuals who emphasise form over content or substance in their approach to various disciplines, such as art, literature, architecture, or philosophy.

### Key Characteristics:

- **Emphasis on Structure:**
    - Focus on the arrangement and organization of elements within a work.
- **Technical Analysis:**
    - Detailed examination of techniques and methods used in creating the work.
- **De-emphasis on Content:**
    - Less concern with the thematic, narrative, or symbolic meanings.
