Teachers use the _Concept Mastery Routine_ to identify characteristics of an example of a concept class that must be always, sometimes, and never(teaching what the concept is not about) present to fit in the concept class. New examples are explored to confirm understanding of a concept.

1. Identify the Concept
2. Determine Critical Characteristics
3. Provide Examples and Non-examples
