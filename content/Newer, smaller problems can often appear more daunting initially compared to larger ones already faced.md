Vaalkaila epome pudhiya vishyam na oru bayam irukum. But once palakka pattutom na, adhu romba easy ah irukum.

Palakka patta romba kodurama vishyam may look easy for us than a palakka padatha non kodooramana vishyam. 

But andha pudhiya vishyam prachanaiya vandha namakku innum bayam varum. 

Andha prachanaiyoda actual size ah vida adhu perusa teriyarathuku karam, adhu new vishyam dradhudhan.
