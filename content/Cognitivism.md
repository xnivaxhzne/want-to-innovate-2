> Metaphor used for Brain - Human mind as a Computer

The information comes in processed and then leads to certain outcomes.  

It is believed that learning is an internal process. It focuses on how the brain works. Its a major leap from what behaviourists believe. 

Learning occurs when the student re-organises information, either by finding new explanations or adapting old ones.

Learns learn the best if we create our lesson, topic, subject in a meaningful and memorable way.
