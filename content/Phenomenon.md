> Phenomenon is an observable event or occurrence in the natural world. It is something that happens and can be perceived.

> [!example] Gravitational Lensing
>  Gravitational lensing is the bending of light from a distant object, such as a star or galaxy, caused by the gravitational field of a massive object between the light source and the observer. This bending can result in magnified, distorted, or multiple images of the distant object.

> a phenomenon is an observable event, a scientific theory provides an explanation of why it occurs, and a scientific law describes what happens, often in a precise mathematical form, without necessarily explaining the underlying cause.
