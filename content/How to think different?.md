In order for your imaginations to operate at peak levels, however, those nuerons have to fire at maximum output. So, bombard your brain with new experiences. 

Use analogies and metaphors to initiate a creative process and to think differently about a problem. By finding the similarities between two things that are unalike, your brain makes new and sometimes profound connections. Using analogies is an effective technique to help listeners understand new concepts. Seeing something novel in a familiar light allows him to make creative connections. 

Keep in mind that your brain will fight you every step of the way. By keeping in mind, you can understand to overcome it.
