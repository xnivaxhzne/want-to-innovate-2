> More aesthetic designs are `perceived`(whether or not they are easier to use) as easier to use than less-aesthetic designs.

- It can make people `tolerant` of design issues.
- It is common for people to name and develop feelings towards designs that fostered positive attitudes(Ex: `naming a car`)
- Such positive feelings evoke affection, loyalty, and patience.
- Positive relationships with a [[Design|design]] result in an interaction that helps catalyse [[Levels of creative thinking -> Discover, Produce, Refine, Prepare, Combine|creative thinking]] and [[Problem Solving|problem solving]].
- Negative relationships result in an interaction that narrows thinking and stifles creativity.
- This is especially important in stressful environments, since stress increases fatigue and reduces [[Human cognition|cognitive]] performance.
