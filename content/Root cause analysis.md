Before finding solutions, we need to understand the root of the problem. Identifying the `essential purpose of an undertaking` is key to solving it. The first step is to determine [[Why before What and What before How|what the endeavour is supposed to accomplish and why it's necessary]]. This clarity is where the solution lies.

> [!example]
> - Learning should be the focus, not just the presence of schools.
> - Safety should be prioritized over the number of police officers.
> - Mobility is the goal, not just building more highways.
> - Communication is key, not just putting up more signs.
