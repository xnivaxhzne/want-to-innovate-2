Evidentiality:

Making it clear when you are telling something that you saw with your own eyes vs repeating something that someone else told you.

If you saw the event with your own eye, you add a certain suffix to the verb.

> She kicked `uh` him.

If someone told you the story, you add a different suffix to the verb.

> She kicked `ay` him.
