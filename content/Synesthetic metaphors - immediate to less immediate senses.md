Ability to perceive a stimulus in one sense organ through a different sensory system as well.
Some synesthetes perceive taste when they see shape; others hear sounds when they see numbers.

Along with [[Pattern|pattern recognition]], synesthesia may be one of the neurological building blocks of metaphors. 


> Synesthetic metaphors follow a remarkably consistent pattern: Words derived from immediate senses describe the experience of less immediate senses - [[Classification of senses]]
