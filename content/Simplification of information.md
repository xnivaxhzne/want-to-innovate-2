Simplification of information may imply qualitative reduction but sometimes more information means more clarity. So it is highly subjective.

Simplicity can be easy when keeping the following 5 designs in mind:
1. Tell the truth
2. Get to the point
3. Pick the right tool for the job
4. [[Highlighting|Highlight]] what is important
5. Of course, keep it simple.

> Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication is highlighted - a very Asian concept.

Complex information should be presented in the simplest form, so that the focus is on the information rather than the way it is presented.
